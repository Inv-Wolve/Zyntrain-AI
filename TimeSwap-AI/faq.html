Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Zyntrain AI

How do I get started with Zyntrain AI?

Getting started is simple! Click "Get Started" on our homepage, create your free account, and you'll be taken to your dashboard. From there, you can add your first tasks and let our AI start optimizing your schedule.

Is Zyntrain AI really free?

Yes! Zyntrain AI is completely free forever. We believe everyone deserves access to powerful productivity tools. All features including AI optimization, calendar integration, and analytics are included at no cost.

Do I need to download any software?

No downloads required! Zyntrain AI runs entirely in your web browser. Simply visit our website and start using it immediately. It works on all modern browsers and devices.

How does the AI task prioritization work?

Our AI analyzes multiple factors including deadlines, your energy patterns, task complexity, and historical completion data. It then suggests the optimal order for your tasks to maximize productivity and reduce stress.

Can I integrate with Google Calendar?

Yes! Zyntrain AI seamlessly integrates with Google Calendar. Your existing meetings and events are automatically considered when the AI optimizes your schedule, ensuring no conflicts occur.

What is energy-based scheduling?

Energy-based scheduling matches your tasks with your natural energy levels throughout the day. High-energy tasks are scheduled during your peak hours, while routine tasks are placed during lower-energy periods.

How many AI chat messages can I send per day?

You can send up to 10 AI chat messages per day. This limit helps us maintain service quality while keeping the platform free for everyone. The limit resets every 24 hours.

What can I ask the AI assistant?

You can ask about task prioritization, schedule optimization, productivity tips, time management strategies, and get personalized recommendations based on your work patterns and preferences.

How does the AI learn my patterns?

The AI observes your task completion patterns, preferred working hours, energy levels, and how you respond to suggestions. Over time, it becomes more accurate at predicting what works best for you.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption and security measures. Your data is stored securely and never shared with third parties. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.

Can I export my data?

Yes! You can export all your data at any time from your profile settings. We believe in data portability and your right to access your information.

Do you use my data to train AI models?

No, we do not use your personal data to train AI models. Your tasks, schedules, and personal information remain private and are only used to provide you with personalized recommendations.

What browsers are supported?

Zyntrain AI works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend using the latest version of your preferred browser for the best experience.

Does it work on mobile devices?

Yes! Zyntrain AI is fully responsive and works great on smartphones and tablets. You can access all features from any device with an internet connection.

What happens if I lose internet connection?

Zyntrain AI automatically syncs your data when you reconnect. Any changes made offline will be saved locally and synchronized once your connection is restored.

How often does the data sync?

Data syncs automatically every time you make changes. Additionally, the system performs background syncs every 5 minutes to ensure your data is always up to date across all your devices.
